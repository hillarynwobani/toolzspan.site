# Product Hunt Launch Kit for Toolzspan

## Launch Day Post

### Name (Headline)
Toolzspan — 50 free online tools for PDF, video, audio, images

### Tagline (One-liner below headline)
No sign-up. No upload to remote servers. No limits. Works in your browser.

### Description (Full text)

Every day, millions of people need to compress a PDF, convert a video, resize an image, or generate a QR code. Most tools online either force you to create an account, push a paid subscription, or upload your files to a remote server.

Toolzspan does none of that.

We built 50 free online tools that run directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device. There is no account to create, no subscription to manage, and no hidden limits.

**What you can do:**
- **PDF:** Compress, merge, split, convert to Word, add password, remove password, edit, OCR, sign, add watermark
- **Image:** Convert between JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC; compress; resize; convert to/from PDF
- **Video & Audio:** Convert, compress, trim between MP4, MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, AVI, MOV, WebM
- **GIF:** Create from video, convert, compress
- **General:** QR code generator, word counter, color picker, base64 encoder, units converter, time converter

**Why people use it:**
- Zero sign-up friction
- 100% private — files stay on your device
- Works on any device with a browser
- Completely free, no premium tier
- Clean, fast, no popups

Built by a web engineer since 2018. 500,000+ users served. 10 million+ files processed.

### Maker Comment (First Comment After Posting)

Hi Product Hunt!

I built Toolzspan because I was tired of landing on "free" tool sites that demanded my email, pushed a subscription, or uploaded my files to who-knows-where.

I would love to hear what tools you use most, what is missing, and what we can add next. All feedback welcome.
