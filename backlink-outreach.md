Toolzspan is a free, browser-based utility platform with 50 tools for everyday file tasks. No account required. No file uploads to external servers. No limits.

Categories covered:
- PDF: compress, merge, split, convert, edit, password-protect
- Image: convert, compress, resize (JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC)
- Video & Audio: convert, compress, trim (MP4, MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC)

**Key differentiators:**
- Zero sign-up friction
- 100% private (files stay on your device)
- Works on any device with a browser
- Completely free, no premium tier

## Tips

- **Personalize every email.** Mention a specific post or detail. Mass emails get ignored.
- **Lead with value.** Do not ask for a link — offer something useful first.
- **Be brief.** 3-4 short paragraphs max.
- **Follow up once, maybe twice.** Then move on.
- **Track your outreach.** Use a simple spreadsheet: site, contact, date sent, status.
