How to Use Gif Maker

  1. Click the upload area or drag and drop multiple images (JPG, PNG, etc.).
  2. Arrange the images in the order you want them to appear.
  3. Set the frame delay (speed) for your animation.
  4. Click "Create GIF" to generate your animated GIF.
  5. Download your new GIF file.

Why Use Gif Maker?

  • Create from Images — Turn a series of photos or screenshots into an animated GIF.
  • Custom Speed — Control how fast or slow the animation plays.
  • No App Needed — Create GIFs directly in your browser without installing software.
  • Share Anywhere — GIFs work in texts, emails, social media, and on the web.
