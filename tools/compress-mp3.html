How to Use Compress Mp3

  1. Click the upload area or drag and drop your MP3 file.
  2. Select your target bitrate — lower bitrate means smaller file.
  3. Click "Compress" to re-encode at the chosen bitrate.
  4. Wait while the MP3 is processed.
  5. Download your smaller MP3 file.
