How to Use Trim Audio

  1. Click the upload area or drag and drop your audio file (MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A, AAC).
  2. Wait for the waveform to load — this takes a few seconds depending on file size.
  3. Drag the highlighted region handles left or right to select the portion you want to keep.
  4. Click the play button to preview your selection before trimming.
  5. Click "Trim Audio" to process, then download your trimmed clip.

Why Use Trim Audio?

  • Create Ringtones — Cut your favorite section from any song to make a custom ringtone.
  • Edit Podcasts — Remove intros, outros, or awkward pauses from podcast recordings.
  • Extract Clips — Pull out a specific quote, sound effect, or music sample from a longer recording.
  • 100% Private — Everything runs in your browser. Your audio never touches a server.
