How to Use Audio Compressor

  1. Click the upload area or drag and drop your audio file (MP3, WAV, etc.).
  2. Select your desired output quality or bitrate.
  3. Click "Compress" to reduce the file size.
  4. Wait while the audio is re-encoded.
  5. Download your compressed audio file.
